Optimization of NMAP scans

  • there are the nmap timing systems which can used to control the number of packets sent in a certain time, use manpage to learn the things about TIME AND PERFORMANCE.
  • read manpage of nmap
  • you can use --host-timeout flag to leave a host after certain time and move to the next host
  • You can change the time shown in version to UTC or other in preferences.
